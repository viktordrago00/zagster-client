This data visualization is meant to be a visual breakdown of the total number of rides that were taken on Zagster bikes in Bend since September of 2016. The total number of rides taken since September of 2016 is listed in green above this paragraph. The pie chart below shows the total number of rides broken down by year, and then by month. The inner most part of the pie chart shows the number of rides per year in the format "YEAR: NUMBER OF RIDES". The outer ring of the pie chart shows the number of rides per month in the format "MONTH/YEAR: NUMBER OF RIDES" . If you look at the chart as a clock, the dates get more recent as you go clockwise starting from 12 o'clock. This is true for both rides per year and rides per month.
